IUL Education

What Is Indexed Universal Life Insurance?

Indexed Universal Life, or IUL, is a type of permanent life insurance that provides a death benefit and may also build cash value over time. The cash value growth may be linked to a market index, but the policyholder is not directly investing in the stock market.

Learn More About IUL
1. IUL can offer flexibility.

Policy features may allow flexible premiums and long-term protection planning when structured properly.

2. IUL must be properly funded.

Underfunding a policy can create problems. Review funding assumptions with a licensed professional.

3. IUL is not right for everyone.

Suitability depends on goals, budget, time horizon, health, product availability, and risk tolerance.

4. IUL is not guaranteed wealth.

It should not be described as risk-free, guaranteed income, or a shortcut to wealth.
